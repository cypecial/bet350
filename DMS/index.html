Our Passion

Diamant Modular Solutions are modular rooms that fit into existing locations to provide employees with a private, clean space to decompress. Designed with professionals in mind, Diamant Mods are soundproof, easily assembled, and include a variety of features.

Features

Soundproof

Soundproof walls to protect your privacy

Mental Health Support

Mental health resources include meditative and breathing exercises, information sheets, and the ability to call a counsellor right to your room

Flexible furniture

Able to transforms to suit different needs

Control Center

Central control unit controls light, temperature, ambient noise

Automatic Lock

Activation of control unit locks the door to ensure constant privacy

On-site booking

Rooms can be booked from a tablet right outside the door

Always cleaned

Cleaning services are notified when the room is vacated to ensure the spaces are sanitary

Control Hub

Our central control hub has many components to ensure the comfort of our clients. When you tap into the room with your staff card, the control hub tailors the experience to your past preferences. To ensure client confidentiality, however, that’s all it remembers - it won’t track if you contact a counsellor from the room.

Comfortable, Adaptable Space

Our rooms are flexible to ensure that they suit the needs of our clients: whether they need a nap, a quiet moment, or to take an important phone call, our spaces adapt.
